Interactive Academia Library

  • Our Approach

    Interactive Academia Library is the complete solution for easy access to Cambridge IGCSE textbooks.

    IA Library is easy to use, accessible to students, and unswerving for any classroom.

  • Our Purpose

    Many students have problems accessing their required IGCSE textbooks. IA Library solves this problem because it gives students one place to access al the textbooks, at any time.

  • Solution

    This has been a problem for students of ACA for a long time, and greatly hinders the students of ACA.

    Due to this fact, we have decided to make a change, and help students with any problems they may have with accessing their required textbooks.
